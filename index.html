About Me

A sunset at the Grand Canyon. Totally worth it!

I can be described that bites of more than he can chew, metaphorically and literally! It is better to be a moderately large fish in a big pond than a goldfish in a fishbowl. It is this mentality that got me though my undergraduate and graduate years. I would also like to thank all people who gave me free food! I couldn't have done it without you.

My name is John Niedfeldt, and that is pronounced "Need-felt". I cannot tell you how many times people have mispronounced my name. Recently, I even considered changing my last name, though I have a hard time thinking what name I should grace with my association. Maybe Smith? Except for the fact that everyone would associate me with the default form text on the internet, or Pocahontas' lover in the Disney movie, it would be great!

My free time is spent hanging out with friends, watching movies, binging Netflix, reading books, playing Ultimate (when I can find people to play with), keeping up on the newest technologies, programming in Python or other programming languages, or having deep discussions with my close friends. I also just switched careers and am now a Software Developer for a cyber security startup called Avirtek. I have no idea where I am going to be in 10 years, but I know that the journey will be an adventure of epic proportions, and I am the captain of my fate.
